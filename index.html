Research

Exploring innovative solutions at the intersection of technology and social impact.

AI Companions as Non-Judgmental Allies

Democratizing Financial and Mental Health Support for Underserved Communities. Financial burdens significantly worsen mental health amongst low-income populations, creating a devastating cycle that's difficult to break. I'm investigating how AI companions can be used to lessen the financial burden and therefore address mental health issues, particularly because low-income communities don't have adequate access to these critical resources. By providing non-judgmental, accessible support through AI, we can begin to bridge this gap and offer meaningful assistance to those who need it most.

Initiatives I've Started

Connect&Expand

I was inspired to offer business-related internship and shadowing opportunities after seeing the lack of them offered and the hesitance girls in my community had pursuing finance-related careers. Through Connect&Expand, I'm creating pathways for young women to explore business and finance through meaningful mentorship and real-world experience. This image shows the early stages of us planning Connect&Expand and seeing what opportunities FBLA members were interested in.

STEMPowerHer Hackathon

I realized that underserved communities across the globe have unique problems that only they themselves can solve because they have experienced them firsthand. To motivate girls to explore business and AI to create socially impactful solutions, I started this hackathon. Over 85 signups internationally and growing!

STEMPowerHer International Community Journal

I realized that STEM & AI education isn't prevalent worldwide, so I wanted to expand access to help girls realize what careers interested them and connect like-minded individuals. Through this journal, we're building a global community of young women passionate about STEM. Almost 30 signups internationally and growing!
